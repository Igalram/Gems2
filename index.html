Art Museums' Digital Collectibles

Culture Gems is a REVOLUTIONARY Blockchain-based platform that allows art museums worldwide to offer UNIQUE, COLLECTIBLE, and TRADABLE digital tokens (Gems) from the items on their vast collection.

Art Museum Facts

Art Museums

Art museums are a huge global industry. The market assessed at 3B USD industry in the US alone. Thousands of museums worldwide.

99% Are struggling

99% of them are struggling financially. Out of hundreds of museums, only a handful of "celebrity" museums are faring well financially (mainly thanks to invested endowments).

Philantropy has shifted

Museums typically rely on philantropy for 25% of their annual income. However, donors and corporate charity allocations are looking for impact investments

Revenues low

Revenues from admissions and stores are low, sometimes lower than 20%. Conflicting KPI's for state and municipal support

Public funds demand KPIs

State and municipal participation, that exceed 25% of many museums budgets, demand relevance and clearer performance indicators for continued subsidy

3% in display

At the same time, only a fraction of many museums' vast collections are on display, with some works never displayed at all.

The Solution

Culture Gems is a platform that utilizes, gamifies and monetizes museums' vast collections in a novel way that creates a new source of funding and engagement to museums. We harness Blockchain technology and high volume-VC backed marketing to facilitate a lively arena for collecting and trading unique, one-of digital art cards from the museums collections.

How it works?

Art museums sign up with CultureGems and offer quality digital copies of their collections on the Culture Gems blockchain network. Funds paid will be divided between the Museum (and recognized as donations) and Culture Gems.

Culture Gems will use the income to continously auction and market additional series of works, sorted by themes, genres, periods, movements and individual artists.

Collectors can focus on series or on individual museums, discover additional art, bid on the primary offers, trade and engage with other collectors. They can also make offers on specific opportunities within the museum, or at the museum stores
